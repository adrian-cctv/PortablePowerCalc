Design Step: Finding Sun Hours

To ensure 24/7 uptime, you must design for the worst-case month (Winter Solstice).

  1. Visit PVWatts Calculator.
  2. Enter address & click Go. Navigate to Results.
  3. Find the lowest Solar Radiation value (e.g., June = 3.79).
  4. Enter that value in the Worst-Month PSH field below.
